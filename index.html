The fixed-radius near neighbor is a O(n + k) algorithm, where k is the number of pairs to get reported. Note that if k < n2 then this algorithm should perform better than the naive O(n2) approach.

Further Reading:

  • "The complexity of finding fixed-radius near neighbors" by JL Bentley, DF Stanat, and EH Williams
  • Lecture 1 of Computational Geometry by D Mount for the proof of the runtime and a more intuitive read
